- Warm and authentic
- Professional yet nurturing
- Evidence-based yet spiritual
- Community-focused
- Supportive of growth

- Meaningful practitioner-seeker matching through archetypes
- Carefully verified practitioners
- Nature-inspired approach to wellness
- Supportive community for new facilitators (Bee pathway)
